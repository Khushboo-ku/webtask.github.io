Robortic
In technology, a robot is a technological machine or system designed to carry out tasks automatically or with minimal human intervention. Robots are a major part of modern technology and combine mechanical engineering, electronics, computer science, and artificial intelligence (AI).

Artificial Intelligence (AI)
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the technology that allows machines or computers to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. It helps machines solve problems, recognize patterns, and even understand language or images.
